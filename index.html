Research Focus Areas

Investigating the psychological and behavioral factors that influence tornado risk perception

Cognitive Biases

Examining how mental shortcuts and biases affect tornado risk assessment and decision-making processes.

  • Availability heuristic effects
  • Optimism bias patterns
  • Risk normalization tendencies

Social Influences

Understanding how community dynamics, social networks, and cultural factors shape tornado risk perception.

  • Peer influence on preparedness
  • Community resilience factors
  • Information sharing patterns

Communication Effectiveness

Evaluating how different warning systems and communication strategies impact public response.

  • Warning message clarity
  • Multi-channel communication
  • Response time optimization

Geographic Factors

Analyzing how location-specific variables influence tornado risk awareness and preparedness levels.

  • Regional risk variations
  • Historical impact correlation
  • Terrain influence analysis
